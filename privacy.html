Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Welcome to VEXO. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use VEXO, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address when you create an account.
  • Fitness Data: Workout sessions, exercise logs, sets, reps, weights, and personal records.
  • Body Metrics: Bodyweight entries you manually log in the app.
  • App Preferences: Fitness level, weight unit preference, and active training plan.

We do not collect payment information, location data, contacts, or any data from your device beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize your training experience.
  • To calculate streaks, activity rings, and progress statistics.
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase cloud storage.
  • To improve the app's features and performance.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage & Third-Party Services

VEXO uses Firebase (a Google LLC service) for authentication and cloud data storage. Your data is stored on Google's secure servers. Firebase is subject to Google's Privacy Policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Your data is also stored locally on your device using SQLite and AsyncStorage for offline access.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your personal data — including workout history, personal records, and profile information — is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data directly from the app (Profile → Delete Account).
  • Export your data upon request by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

VEXO is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including Firebase Authentication, encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), and secure local storage. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
