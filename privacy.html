Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Welcome to VEXO. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. This policy applies to all users worldwide, including users in the European Union, United States, Brazil, and other jurisdictions.

1. Information We Collect

When you use VEXO, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address when you create an account.
  • Fitness Data: Workout sessions, exercise logs, sets, reps, weights, and personal records.
  • Body Metrics: Bodyweight entries you manually log in the app.
  • App Preferences: Fitness level, weight unit preference, and active training plan.
  • Technical Data: Anonymous crash reports and performance data collected via Sentry.

We do not collect payment information, location data, contacts, or any device data beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide and personalize your training experience.
  • To calculate streaks, activity rings, and progress statistics.
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase.
  • To send workout reminder notifications (only if you enable them).
  • To diagnose and fix app crashes via anonymous error reporting.
  • To improve the app's features and performance.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

GDPR — EU / EEA Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data under the following legal bases (GDPR Art. 6):

  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing your account and fitness data is necessary to provide the VEXO service.
  • Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): Anonymous crash reporting to maintain a stable, secure app.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Push notifications — only sent if you grant permission. You can withdraw consent at any time in your device settings.

4. Session Recording & Diagnostics

TRANSPARENCY NOTICE

To help us identify and fix bugs, VEXO may capture anonymous screen recordings of user interactions for approximately 10% of sessions using Sentry's Session Replay feature. These recordings:

  • Are used exclusively for debugging and error diagnosis — never for advertising or profiling.
  • Do not contain personally identifiable information (no names, emails, or user IDs are linked to recordings).
  • Are automatically deleted after 30 days.
  • Are processed by Sentry.io, which is GDPR-compliant and SOC 2 certified.
  • Are not shared with any third party beyond Sentry.io.

If you are an EU/EEA user and wish to opt out of session recordings, please contact us at vexoapp1@gmail.com and we will disable recording for your account.

5. Data Storage & Third-Party Services

VEXO uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google LLC): Authentication and cloud storage. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Sentry: Anonymous crash reporting and session diagnostics (see Section 4 above). No personally identifiable data is included. Subject to Sentry's Privacy Policy.

Your data is also stored locally on your device using SQLite and AsyncStorage for offline access.

6. International Data Transfers

GDPR — EU / EEA Users

Your data may be stored on servers in the United States where Firebase (Google) operates. Google LLC complies with the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework, ensuring your data receives adequate protection under GDPR.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. Anonymous Sentry crash data is deleted after 90 days.

  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw notification consent any time in device settings.
